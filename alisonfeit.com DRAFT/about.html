About Me

Hi! I'm Alison.

I am a Harvard and Yale trained psychologist with extensive experience working in the United States and globally. I offer tailored support and interventions for influential decision-makers, pioneering entrepreneurs, and forward-thinking industry trailblazers.

Faculty & Professor

  • Harvard University: Awarded the university's top teaching award in recognition of demonstrated excellence and outstanding contributions in psychological insight and personality change.
  • Chinese-American Psychoanalytic Alliance: I have served as a faculty member and professor with the Chinese-American Psychoanalytic Alliance, contributing to the education and training of psychoanalysts and mental health professionals. Specialized cross-cultural courses for top-level corporate executives, entrepreneurial leaders, and industry innovators.
  • St. Louis Psychoanalytic Institute: Faculty member specializing in advanced training in psychoanalytic theory and practice for mental health professionals as well as practical courses in personality change and psychological growth for the general public.
  • Stony Brook Business School: Served as a professor specializing in the integration of psychological principles into the unique needs and challenges faced by senior corporate executives, prominent business innovators, and transformative industry pioneers.

Education

  • W.A. White Institute of Psychiatry - Post Doctorate, Certificate in Adult Psychoanalysis, Psychoanalysis & Psychology: I completed a 5-year postdoctoral program at the W.A. White Institute of Psychiatry, earning a certificate in Adult Psychoanalysis, with a focus on the mechanism needed for deep psychological change and interpersonal growth.
  • Adelphi University - PhD in Psychology: I conducted extensive research on the intricate neural mechanisms responsible for forging our distinctive character and subtly compel us to make decisions beyond our explicit control.
  • Harvard University - Master of Arts in Psychology: I obtained my Master of Arts (MA) in Psychology from Harvard University, furthering my expertise in psychological theories and practices.
  • Yale University - Bachelor of Arts in Psychology: I completed my undergraduate education at Yale University, earning a Bachelor of Arts (BA) with honors in Psychology, which laid the foundation for my advanced studies and professional career.

Here are a few key aspects of my approach as a corporate coach:

Immediate Impact

I prioritize delivering quick, effective strategies to alleviate stress and enhance performance. In high-pressure corporate environments, timely relief is crucial for maintaining productivity and well-being.

Deep Understanding

I believe that challenges often reveal deeper insights into an individual's strengths, motivations, and areas for growth. By addressing these underlying factors, we can develop tailored strategies that lead to sustainable success.

Balanced Perspective

While I tackle serious issues with the gravity they deserve, I also incorporate humor and positivity into my practice. This balance helps to create a dynamic and engaging environment which fosters resilience and innovation.
