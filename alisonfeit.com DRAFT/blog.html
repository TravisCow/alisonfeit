Hi! I'm Alison.

I am a Harvard and Yale trained psychologist with extensive experience working globally to enhance well-being, mentor executives and entrepreneurs, mediate business disputes, and engage in writing and teaching.

Faculty & Professor

  • Chinese-American Psychoanalytic Alliance: I have served as a faculty member and professor with the Chinese-American Psychoanalytic Alliance, contributing to the education and training of psychoanalysts and mental health professionals.
  • Harvard University: As a faculty member at Harvard University, I have been involved in teaching and mentoring students in the field of psychology.
  • St. Louis Psychoanalytic Institute: At the St. Louis Psychoanalytic Institute, I have held a faculty position, providing advanced training in psychoanalytic theory and practice.
  • Stony Brook Business School: I have also been a professor at the Stony Brook Business School, integrating psychological principles into business education and leadership development.

Education

  • W.A. White Institute of Psychiatry - Post Doctorate, Certificate in Adult Psychoanalysis, Psychoanalysis & Psychology: I completed a postdoctoral program at the W.A. White Institute of Psychiatry, earning a certificate in Adult Psychoanalysis, with a focus on Psychoanalysis and Psychology.
  • Adelphi University - PhD in Psychology: I earned my Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Psychology from Adelphi University, where I conducted extensive research and gained in-depth knowledge in the field.
  • Harvard University - Master of Arts in Psychology: I obtained my Master of Arts (MA) in Psychology from Harvard University, furthering my expertise in psychological theories and practices.
  • Yale University - Bachelor of Arts in Psychology: I completed my undergraduate education at Yale University, earning a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Psychology, which laid the foundation for my advanced studies and professional career.

Here are a few key aspects of my approach as a corporate coach:

Immediate Impact

I prioritize delivering quick, effective strategies to alleviate stress and enhance performance. In high-pressure corporate environments, timely relief is crucial for maintaining productivity and well-being.

Deep Understanding

I believe that challenges often reveal deeper insights into an individual's strengths, motivations, and areas for growth. By addressing these underlying factors, we can develop tailored strategies that lead to sustainable success.

Balanced Perspective

While I tackle serious issues with the gravity they deserve, I also incorporate humor and positivity into my coaching. This balance helps create a dynamic and engaging coaching environment that fosters resilience and innovation.
